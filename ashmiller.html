About

Professional, prepared, easy to work with.

Originally from Hungary, Ash moved to London to push his music career further after multiple stints touring Europe with the circus.

That background helped Ash build consistency under pressure — walking into unfamiliar setups, adapting fast, staying solid night after night.

Ash is also an avid dubstep producer, deeply embedded in London’s electronic music scene.

Since relocating, he’s worked with metal and rock acts across the London scene and beyond. He’s known for being dependable, easy to work with, and a friendly presence in the community — the sort of person you can trust on a show day.

Offstage: gym sessions, craft beers, and Guinness (especially Guinness).
