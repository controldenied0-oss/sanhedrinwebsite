BIO

Sanhedrin emerged in 2009, fusing death metal and melodic black metal into their own unique sound. In 2010, they unleashed their debut album, 'Salvation Through Sin', followed by the defining EP 'And On Into The Eternal Nether...' in 2011.

After a prolonged silence, Sanhedrin has risen once again in 2021 with a new line-up, currently weaving their first new material since their resurrection.
